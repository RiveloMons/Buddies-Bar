Our Story

Good vibes &
great cocktails

Buddies Bar is the friendliest cocktail bar in Tenerife. Located in the heart of Los Cristianos, we serve hand-crafted, unique cocktails and mocktails made with the freshest ingredients — always with a smile.

Our Cherry Moonshine is legendary on the island. Taste just like a cherry Haribo sweet, it's our #1 seller and an original Buddies creation. Come up to our Top Sun Deck rooftop for the best views of Los Cristianos.
